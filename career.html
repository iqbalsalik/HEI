Sales Representative

Join HEI, a leading provider of electrical automation solutions, as a Sales Representative

and play a key role in driving our business growth. As a sales representative, you will be responsible for identifying new sales opportunities, building strong relationships with customers, and promoting our innovative electrical automation products and services. If you have a passion for sales, a strong understanding of the electrical automation industry, and excellent communication skills, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity to join our dynamic team and contribute to our company's success.

Field Service Engineer

As a Field Service Engineer at HEI, you will be the face of our company, delivering exceptional technical support and service to our customers in the field. Your expertise in

electrical automation will enable you to troubleshoot, repair, and maintain our cutting-edge systems, ensuring maximum uptime and customer satisfaction. With a strong focus on safety, quality, and customer service, you will work independently and as part of a team to resolve complex technical issues, providing timely and effective solutions that meet our customers' needs. If you're a skilled engineer with a passion for problem-solving and a commitment to excellence, we invite you to join our team and take your career to the next level.

Engineering

Join HEI's innovative engineering team and play a crucial role in designing, developing, and delivering cutting-edge electrical automation solutions. As an Engineer, you will collaborate with cross-functional teams

to create and implement new products, systems, and processes that meet our customers' evolving needs. With a focus on technical excellence, creativity, and problem-solving, you will work on challenging projects that require expertise in electrical engineering, automation, and control systems. If you're a motivated and talented engineer looking to make a meaningful impact in a dynamic and growing company, we encourage you to apply and become part of our team of innovators.
