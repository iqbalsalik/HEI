Annual Maintenance Contracts

At HEI, we offer comprehensive support to ensure your systems run smoothly and efficiently. Our expert technicians perform routine system checkups and health

assessments to identify potential issues before they become major problems. We also conduct thorough panel wiring and connection inspections to guarantee reliable connections. Additionally, we provide data and software backups to safeguard your critical information. Our team of experts will provide personalized recommendations to optimize your systems' performance. And, in the event of an unexpected breakdown, our on-site support team is ready to respond quickly and get you back up and running with minimal downtime.

Product Commissioning

Our product commissioning service ensures a seamless and efficient setup of your equipment. We start with expert installation, followed by rigorous functional testing to verify

performance. Our team then integrates the equipment into your existing systems, guaranteeing a harmonious and productive workflow. Next, we perform precise performance calibration to optimize output and productivity. Safety checks are also conducted to identify and mitigate potential risks. Finally, we provide comprehensive documentation, including user manuals, technical specifications, and maintenance guides, to empower your team with the knowledge they need to operate and maintain the equipment effectively.

Site Commissioning

Our comprehensive site commissioning service ensures a seamless and efficient setup of your equipment. We begin with Factory Acceptance Testing (FAT) and Site Acceptance

Testing (SAT) to verify performance. Our expert technicians then handle cable laying and termination, followed by the installation of electrical and control panels. Next, we perform cold commissioning to test systems without power, followed by hot commissioning to test systems under operational conditions. A thorough performance test is conducted to ensure optimal output. Additionally, we offer system upgradation services to enhance existing infrastructure. Finally, we complete a thorough project handover activity to ensure a smooth transition, empowering your team with the knowledge and support needed to operate and maintain the equipment effectively.

After Sales Services

Our comprehensive after-sales services ensure uninterrupted operation and optimal performance of your equipment. We offer expert installation and commissioning,

followed by on-site or remote support for prompt assistance. Our warranty support provides peace of mind, while our standard spare parts and preventive maintenance kits ensure timely maintenance. Our skilled technicians perform troubleshooting and repairs, as well as inspection and maintenance, to prevent unexpected downtime. Furthermore, we offer customized service contracts tailored to meet individual customer requirements, including maintenance contracts, drive care contracts, and extended contracts, providing flexibility and convenience. With our after-sales services, you can rely on us to support your equipment throughout its lifecycle.

Repairing Services

Our expert repairing services cover a wide range of equipment, including AC, DC, and servo drive repair, as well as in-house repair of UPS and SMPS

systems. Our skilled technicians also specialize in the repair of PLC modules and HMI systems, ensuring minimal downtime for your industrial control systems. Additionally, we offer in-house repair of ACB and VCB systems, as well as drive's PCB repair, and general PCB repair up to component level. For added convenience, we also provide on-site repairing services, allowing us to come to you and repair your equipment in place. With our comprehensive repairing services, you can trust us to get your equipment up and running efficiently and effectively.
