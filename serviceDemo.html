Annual Maintenance Contracts

Routine System Checkups: Regular inspections to ensure all systems are operating efficiently, minimizing the risk of unexpected failures.

Health Assessment of Systems: Comprehensive evaluations of your systems to identify and address any potential issues before they become critical.
Panel Wiring and Connection Inspections: Detailed inspections of panel wiring and connections to maintain safety and reliability in your electrical infrastructure.
Data and Software Backups: Secure backup services to protect your critical data and software, ensuring business continuity in case of unforeseen events.
Expert Recommendations: Professional advice and guidance to optimize system performance and prolong equipment lifespan.
On-site Breakdown Support: Immediate on-site assistance to resolve any breakdowns swiftly, minimizing downtime and operational disruption.

Product Commissioning

Installation: Expert installation of your equipment, ensuring proper setup and integration with existing systems.
Functional Testing: Thorough testing of all functionalities to confirm that your

systems are working as intended.
Integration: Seamless integration of new equipment with your existing infrastructure for smooth operation.
Performance Calibration: Precision calibration to optimize the performance of your equipment, ensuring it meets specified standards.
Safety Checks: Comprehensive safety checks to ensure compliance with industry standards and regulations.
Documentation: Complete and detailed documentation of the commissioning process for future reference and compliance purposes.

Site Commissioning

Factory Acceptance Test (FAT): Pre-installation testing at the factory to ensure the equipment meets all design specifications before delivery.


Site Acceptance Test (SAT): On-site testing to verify that the installed systems perform according to the agreed specifications.
Cable Laying and Termination: Professional installation and termination of cables to ensure reliable connections and signal integrity.
Installation of Electrical and Control Panels: Precise installation of electrical and control panels for optimal system performance.
Cold Commissioning: Initial system tests without load to verify proper installation and setup.
Hot Commissioning: Testing systems under operational conditions to confirm their readiness for full-scale operation.
Performance Testing: Rigorous testing to ensure your systems meet performance expectations under real-world conditions.
System Upgradation: Upgrading existing systems to enhance performance, efficiency, and compliance with the latest standards.
Project Handover Activity: Comprehensive handover process, including training and documentation, ensuring a smooth transition to operational use.

Repairing Services (In-House/On-Site)

AC, DC, and Servo Drive Repair: Expert repair services for a wide range of drives, restoring them to optimal working condition.

UPS and SMPS Repair: Professional repair services for Uninterruptible Power Supplies (UPS) and Switch Mode Power Supplies (SMPS), ensuring reliable power management.
