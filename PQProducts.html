PFC Capacitors

The low-voltage capacitor QCap from Hitachi Energy has the following features:
• Dry type design
• Safe sealing design
• Exclusive overpressure disconnection system
• Long lifetime
• Standardized capacitor range in a cylindrical form
• Easy to mount in a capacitor bank
• Flexible: can be mounted in both horizontal or vertical position
• Hitachi Energy in-house metalized film giving excellent dielectric properties
• Optimized thermal dissipation
• Low losses design
• Complies with international standards, CE, CSA and UL marked

Brief performance data
QCap
• Voltage range: From 220 to 690 V
• Frequency: 50 and 60 Hz
• Connection: Three-phase
• Net output power: From 2.5 to 30 kvar
• Tolerance on capacitance: 2.5-12.5 kvar: -5%/+10%, 15-30 kvar: 0%/+10%
• Losses: <0.2 Watt/kvar (dielectric only) <0.5 Watt/kvar (including discharge resistor)
• Discharge resistor: Included. Discharge from Un to 50V in 1 minute
• Maximum permissible current: 1.3 x In for continuous operation
• Tolerance on voltage: 1.1 x Un for maximum 8 hours in every 24 hours (according to IEC 60831)
• Case material: Recyclable aluminum
• Color: Raw aluminum
• Fixing: 1 stud (M12). Recommended torque: 10Nm
• Dimensions (DxH): 90x417 mm
• Weight: 3 kg
• Terminals: Cage screws. Recommended torque: 2Nm
• Minimum distance above unit: 20 mm
• Maximum distance between capacitors: 30 mm
• Earth: Earth connection on the enclosure fixation
• Execution: Indoor use only
• Temperature range: -25°C/+55°C (class D according to IEC60831)
• Altitude: Up to 2000 m without derating
• Protection degree: IP20
• Standards: IEC 60831-1 (2014), IEC 60831-2 (2014), UL 810, CSA C22.2 No 190

Detuned Reactors

Smisen make chokes for operation in reactive power compensation systems to protect capacitive batteries. Together with capacitors they form resonance system which limit amplitude of harmonics currents. There is also a possibility to insert sensors in windings in order to protect the choke from overheating.
Filter Specifications:
• Design : Chokes are produced according to IEC 61558
specifications:
• Winding material : Copper or Aluminium
• Insulation Class : Class F (155 °C)
• Protection Degree : IP00
• Rated Voltage of Capacitor : As per customer specs
• Rated Supply Voltage : As per customer specs
• Resonance Frequency : 7%(fr:189 Hz)/14% (fr:134Hz)
• Output : 2.5 KVAR to 100KVAR
• Thermal cutoff : 135 C

AC & DC Input and Output Line Chowk

Line/load reactors play an important role in your power system. It absorbs many of the power line disturbance, which in returns help protect many of your sensitive equipments. Line/Load reactors is also a reliable filtering solution which can help reduce the harmonic currents, generated by inverters and drives, in your system.

General Specifications :
• Rating Range : 0.15 kW - 2000 kW
• Type : 1-Ph/2-Phase/Three Phase
• Input Voltage : 110/230/415/440/480 V AC (As per customer specs)
• Current Range : 1-4500 Amps
• Imax : 150%
• Impedance : 1.5/2/3/4/5/6/7 %
• Ambient Temp : 40/50/60ºC
